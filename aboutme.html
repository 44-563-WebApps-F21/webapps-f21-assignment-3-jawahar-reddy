Thor: The Avenger

Thor is a God from a world called Asgard who is an Avenger with a tag called God of thunder, lightning, storms and strength. He is the heir to the throne of Asgard, held by his father Odin, and a very powerful warrior. He was given a powerful hammer, called Mjolnir, which allowed him to control storms, blast people with energy, fly, open portals to other dimensions, time travel, transform his appearance, and even kill immortals.
Thor made a name for himself as the mightiest warrior on his homeworld and subsequently became well known for his actions on Earth.
